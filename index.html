Raccateer is a production sim idle game. Take charge of a hoard of raccoons and build your trash empire, while managing their unique quirks.

Trash to Treasure

Run your supply chain from trash to tech to waste and back to trash again!

Cozy Raccoon Capitalism

Manage your storefront of recycled goods in a nonstop loop of production and waste.

Raccoon Phone Addiction

Keep your workers' tiny rabid minds focused on building your empire and not watching half a screen of someone cutting soap.

Pull Off Colorful Combos

Purchase new skins and hats for your furry laborers to optimize their skills and personalities.
